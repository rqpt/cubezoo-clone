Our development process

Discover. Design. Develop.

We use an agile philosophy and scrum technique to develop products, as opposed to the traditional waterfall methods. Our clients can see, touch and test their product during production, instead of waiting for the final product.

01. User Research

We work with our clients to understand their business case and customers. We use popular research methods, such as surveys and interviews, to understand how technology is used by real people.

We target specific users, learn about their needs and frustrations, and use the information to enhance customer satisfaction.

02. Project Analysis

We use the information from user research to find the best rated findings and best user practices. We may even create a persona to assist the creative team when designing for the correct audience. Bundling the client's expectation with the right user experience leads to a well received product.

03. Design & Prototyping

We create wireframes as a basic outline of the product architecture. It conveys, navigation, direction and description of the user interface.

Designing interfaces involves thinking about how users will interact with them and what kind of experiences they will have. We want users to have positive experiences with products that look good and are easy to use. Gamifying can make your design more fun and engaging where appropriate.

Prototyping works best for test cycles as this helps to ensure that the product is correctly understood and user-friendly.

04. Software Development

We choose code frameworks based on what will work best for our client's project. For example, a Flutter app may be less expensive to develop than a Native app. Therefore our strategy is adopt a programming language that is both reliable and scalable.

Our development team are paired in groups. The front-end development team handles the product's user interface and experience, while the backend development team handles the product's functionality.

Developed progress is presented and shared client side weekly to biweekly until the project is completed and geared for launch.

05. Quality assessment

We problem solve any issues that would prevent a perfect launch.

Testing is defined as a software testing type, conducted through scripts with the sole motive of finding errors in code. It deals with tests for the entire web or app solution. It helps to enhance the quality of a product while reducing costs, maximising ROI, and saving development time.

06. Further development

We recommend launching our client's product with a soft launch or pilot first. Also known as an early release. This will help to identify any areas of concern and allow us to improve the product before release.

We analyse our products after launches to make sure they meet our standards. All software we create comes with a maintenance plan that keeps it stable and free of technical issues.
